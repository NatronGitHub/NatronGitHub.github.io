2D Tracking

Track difficult movements with ease with Natron's tacker, based on Blender's 2D tracker. Handle and correct a variety of footage types with single point or perspective tracking. Luminance variation correction and manual keyframes are built-in to help you get the right result.

Rotoscoping

Mask, feather, repeat! Natron ºs rotoscope node allows you to capture sub-pixel details. Each roto point has individual controls for feathering, allowing compositors to capture plate motion blur in different areas of each roto-shape. Roto shapes can be tracked for faster results.

Keying

Nuke provides a wide range of keyers to assist in extracting mattes from images. The familiar Additive, Chroma, and Hue keyers are built-in, as well as Natron's powerful own PIK keyer and a variety of community-created keyers. Tools for refining matte edges and color edges gives you professional results.

Paint

Remove objects, clone backgrounds, and fix it in post with Natron! Natron features a vector-based RotoPaint node, making tasks like object removal, dust removal, and creating garbage mattes a breeze. Paint strokes are available for a variety of brushes. Additionally, per-point and global feather, motion blur, blending modes and individual or hierarchical 2D transformations are fully supported.

Animation

Natron offers a simple and efficient way to manage keyframes with an accurate and intuitive curve editor. You can set expressions on animation curves to create fluid, believable motion for objects. Natron also incorporates a fully featured dope-sheet to view all animations at a glance, or to quickly edit clips and keyframes.
