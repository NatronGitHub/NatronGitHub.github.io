Multi-Channel EXR Support

With Natron's support for OpenEXR, take advantage of nearly every pass that comes out of your renderer of choice. Tweak them to match your backgrounds or use them to create unique stylized looks as part of your CG compositing process.

And it doesn't end there! Natron's true 32-bit colorspace allows you to take advantage of the most image data at your disposal. Now, you can finally work without the fear of posterization!

Stereo compositing

Make eye-popping visual effects with Natron's built-in stereoscopic tools. You get all of Natron's 200+ typical nodes in a stereoscopic project, plus a variety of tools for working with multiple views and previewing stereo live in Natron.
