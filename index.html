Work smarter with nodes

No more hunting through nested pre-comps! Nuke and Fusion users will be right at home with Natron's node-based workflow. Group, merge, and tinker to achieve just about any effect. Create pixel perfect mattes, set-extensions, Hollywood-style VFX and more, all with Natron's offerings.

Build, group, and save your own node snippets to create and share custom tools and presets. Natron features drag-and-drop installation of community-made PyPlugs (Gizmos).
